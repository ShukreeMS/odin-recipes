Lasagna

Per Serving:643 calories; protein 41.3g; carbohydrates 53.4g; fat 29.3g; cholesterol 108.3mg; sodium 707.3mg.

prep: 30 mins cook:30 mins total:1 hr Servings:8 Yield:1 9x13 inch pan

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil
  2. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
